Invidious is an open-source alternative front-end to YouTube. It is incredibly
lightweight, respects your privacy, and allows you to watch videos without being
tracked by Google algorithms.

By using the community-hosted instances listed below, you help distribute the
server load, prevent centralization, and ensure the resilience of the network.
